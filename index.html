Tyre Maintenance Tips

  • Check tyre pressure regularly.
  • Inspect tyres for damage and wear.
  • Rotate your tyres every 5,000 to 7,000 miles.
  • Balance your tyres to prevent uneven wear.
  • Replace tyres when tread depth is below 2/32 of an inch.
